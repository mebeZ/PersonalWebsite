I am the founder and president of the Bronx Science Machine Learning Club. My journey into Machine Learning started in my sophomore year when I entered into the Math & Computer Science research track at Bronx Science (Fall 2017). I learned from my research advisor and graduate students at Columbia about the applications of Machine Learning in research, and started working at Columbia University with Dr. Jiook Cha (Spring 2018). In an effort to create a sustainable long-term foundation for future students, I started the process of getting the BXS Machine Learning Club formally recognized in Spring of my Junior year (Spring 2019). It was an involved process that required approximately six months and number of administrative approvals. We are now a formally approved club! I created our club website BXSML.org and I enjoy writing about deep learning topics here.
