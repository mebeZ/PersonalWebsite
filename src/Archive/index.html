Hello! I'm Zach, a senior at Bronx Science interested in computer science and pursuing scientific research.

Right now, I'm working in the Posner Lab at the Columbia Medical center under the guidance of Dr. Jiook Cha. I am working to better understand the effects of chronic stress on the HPA axis in humans.

During my junior year high school, I wrote an award winning proposal for the MIT competition in which I proposed a novel solution to the scarcity of trained health professionals in developing countries.

I also help organize public kayaking for the New York City community at the Brooklyn Bridge community boathouse by serving as a safety boater. Other than kayaking, I enjoy reading, hiking, and listening to podcasts.
