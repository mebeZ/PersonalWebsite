Projects

Out of more than 250 proposals, mine was one of about a dozen to receive an award.

Raspberry Pi – As a computer enthusiast, I wanted to learn how to make one myself. Using the raspbeery pi kit, I built myself a miniature computer in a matter of weeks.

Line Following Robot – One of my first robotics projects was the creation of a line following robot. I built it from scratch at home over the summer.
